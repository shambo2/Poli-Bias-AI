About Poli-Bias AI

Poli-Bias AI fetches the latest news articles from popular APIs and analyzes them to show a (mock) breakdown of potential bias: Left, Center, and Right. In a production environment, you can replace the mock analysis with your own AI or ML model to gauge an article's political leaning more accurately.
